What Do We Do

Established in 2005 Juman Industrial Investment Company (Juman) is a family-owned business, based in Al Khobar, Saudi Arabia that is focused on investing and operating Joint Ventures and wholly- owned subsidiaries in the Building Material sector.

Juman’s operations extend the Kingdom, the GCC, and neighboring Middle Eastern countries. Juman’s current portfolio includes operating companies that manufacture, supply and install wallsolutions, ceiling systems, roofing products, flooring systems, and building adhesives.
