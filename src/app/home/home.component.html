ABOUT US

Established in 2005 Juman Industrial Investment Company (Juman) is a family-owned business, based in Al Khobar, Saudi Arabia that is focused on investing and operating Joint Ventures and wholly- owned subsidiaries in the Building Material sector. Jumans operations extend the Kingdom

What Do We Do

At Juman, we promote collaborative manufacturing partnerships in building materials and invest in new building solutions and services to complement these partnerships. Juman’s operations extend the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, the GCC, and neighboring Middle Eastern countries. Juman’s current portfolio includes operating companies that manufacture, supply and install wall Solution,Ceiling systems, roofing products, flooring systems,and construction adhesives
